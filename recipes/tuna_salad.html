Tuna Salad Sandwhiches

Description

I'm a huge fan of tuna salad. I personally believe Woden would have loved it as well. They were Norse gods! They must have loved fish.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz. of tuna, chunked and drained.
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 hard boiled egg, chopped
  • 2 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 2 tsp. lemon pepper seasoning
  • Lettuce leaves, curly-leaf
  • 8 Slices of bread

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except bread; mix well. Chill several hours.
  2. Line 4 slices of bread with lettuce; top each with 1/4 tuna mixture, add cheese and or condiments as desired, and top with remaining bread.
