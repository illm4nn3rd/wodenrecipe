Spam and Rice

Description:

Although Woden probably never got to taste the pleasures of an industrialized pallette... I know his men would have surely enjoyed this meal after raiding a coastal village.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of spam
  • one package of microwaveable rice

Steps

  1. Take spam out of can. Cut into desired shape, though bite-size is recommended. Microwave spam on a plate until thoroughly heated.
  2. Put rice in microwave and follow directions on the packaging. When done, eat with spam.
