Japanese curry

Description

Woden would have loved this hearty meal indeed. On a cold night, after a hard day of maining the ship, any seafaring men would definitely enjoy this thick and hardy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of protein, chopped
  • 13 oz of medium onions, minced
  • 3.5 oz of carrot, chopped
  • 5 oz of potato, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 540 ml of Water
  • 1 pack of S&B Golden Curry Mix

Steps

  1. Stir-fry meat and vegetables with oil in a large skillet on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  2. Add water and bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until ingredients are tender, approximately 15 minutes.
  3. Turn the heat off, break curry mix into pieces and add them to the skillet. Stir until sauce mixes are completely melted. Simmer approximately for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

Source: S&B Foods.
